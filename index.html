Overview

CoffeeBits is a collection of 1000 pixel-art NFTs inspired by the comforting hot drinks, the thriving Algo Fam and the groundbreaking Coffee DAO.

Hosted on the Algorand blockchain for its security, speed, low fees and negative carbon footprint.

CoffeeBits is a verified collection on NFTExplorer and RandGallery.

Traits

88 traits have been drawn by hand, pixel by pixel, then brewed with python random magic and selected carefully, resulting in a collection of 1000 unique pictures.

Traits have different rarities, allowing the attribution of a global rarity rank to each NFT (NFTExplorer). Some example of rare traits are: Golden Bean, Yellow Background, ‘Looks Rare’ neon, Crown, Mummy Paper Cup, Golden Mug...
